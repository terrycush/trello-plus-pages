Help

Trello Plus is a Chrome extension that enhances your Trello experience by providing additional features and customization options. Here's how to use Trello Plus:

Popup

  • The extension's popup is located in your browser's toolbar. Click on it to open the popup.
  • The states of the sliders and toggles are global, so they will apply to all Trello boards you are on.

List Width

  • You can adjust the width of the lists by using the slider in the extension's popup.

List Collapse

  • You can collapse and expand lists by clicking on the "Expand" and "Collapse" buttons at the top of the board or by clicking the collapse icon in the top right of the list header.
  • If you click an individual list, the collapse state will be saved for that list only.

Card Count

  • You can show and hide the number of cards in a list by clicking the "Card Count" toggle in the extension's popup.

Card Age

  • You can show and hide the age of the cards in a list by clicking the "Card Age" toggle in the extension's popup.

Card ID

  • You can show and hide the ID of the cards in a list by clicking the "Card ID" toggle in the extension's popup.
